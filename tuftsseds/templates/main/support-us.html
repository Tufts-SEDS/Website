Support Tufts SEDS

Supporting Tufts Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) is a direct investment in the next generation of aerospace leaders, researchers, and innovators. By contributing to Tufts SEDS, you are helping students overcome financial barriers to attend conferences and foster a collaborative environment that encourages learning and networking.

Your donation will help reduce member costs and personal contributions for competitions and conferences, which empower Tufts SEDS to provide valuable resources, mentorship, and support to our members. Together, we can inspire and equip these aspiring engineers and scientists with the tools and skill to shape the future of space exploration and technology. Your generosity makes a tangible impact on the educational journey and professional development of our Tufts SEDS members, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed in their pursuit of knowledge and contribute to the exciting advancements in space.

How to Donate

Check

How to donate via check
Write a check payable to: "Trustees of Tufts College"

Under the memo line, write "Donation: General Tufts SEDS"

Send the check to the M.E. department administrator: "Courtney Russo, Mechanical Engineering 200 College Ave Medford, MA 02155 United States"

The mechanical engineering department will forward the check to advancement, after which the donation will be processed and reserved for the project.

Give Online

How to donate via Tufts's online portal
The following outlines a "giving" via a secure payment form that accepts credit cards, PayPal, and Venmo.
Visit the following website: https://give.tufts.edu

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Make a Gift"
On the form, select "Arts, Sciences & Engineering", then "Other"
You will be asked to specify. Type in "Mechanical Engineering: General Tufts SEDS"

Any funding with the words above specified will be processed and allocated to our project. Congrats, you have successfully donated to our rocket team!

The mechanical engineering department will directly receive the payment, after which the donation will be processed and reserved for the project.
