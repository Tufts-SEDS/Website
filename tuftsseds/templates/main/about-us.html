About Us

At Tufts University, our Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) club offers an inclusive community of enthusiastic aerospace-loving students. As part of our global network of SEDS members, we provide a range of exciting opportunities. These include the chance to develop technical skills, collaborate on team projects, participate in outreach initiatives, connect with industry professionals, attend conferences, and immerse yourself in space-themed events.

Our diverse membership represents various academic backgrounds and experiences, with majors spanning mechanical engineering to astrophysics to electrical engineering to international relations. We welcome anyone with a passion for space!

To join our community, simply attend our general meetings or reach out to us via email at support@sedstufts.org. Ad astra!

We aspire to cultivate within our members:

Community among Jumbos with a shared passion for aerospace and discovery of the unknown. Our chapter aims to foster a welcoming home for aerospace learners and engineers while facilitating an environment that helps students to develop technical, project-based skills through engagement in hands-on projects, engineering competitions, and outreach activities.

Curiosity and the desire to explore aerospace and the region beyond. Our techical projects invoke innovation and creativity, allowing us to develop new technologies and approaches to problem-solving.

Collaboration and interpersonal skills, prioritizing the ability to overcome political, cultural, and linguistic barriers and collaborate towards a shared goal.


Tufts SEDS, a 501(c) nonprofit organization, is affiliated with SEDS-USA, a national organization that empowers young people to participate in the space industry and promote space exploration.
