The Tufts High Altitude Balloon (HAB) team empowers students to explore science and engineering through hands-on stratospheric experiments. By launching custom-built payloads on weather balloons, we test ideas in near-space conditions to turn theory into tangible discoveries. Each mission starts with a question—and ends with data, lessons, and a renewed drive to explore what's next.
