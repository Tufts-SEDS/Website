Leadership

At Tufts University, our Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) club offers an inclusive community of enthusiastic aerospace-loving students. As part of our global network of SEDS members, we provide a range of exciting opportunities. These include the chance to develop technical skills, collaborate on team projects, participate in outreach initiatives, connect with industry professionals, attend conferences, and immerse yourself in space-themed events.

Our diverse membership represents various academic backgrounds and experiences, with majors spanning mechanical engineering, astrophysics, electrical engineering, international relations, mathematics, human factors, computer science, biology, civil engineering, chemistry, physics, chemical engineering, economics, and computer engineering. We welcome anyone with a passion for space!

Our weekly meetings take place in Joyce Cummings Center 260 every Sunday at 1:00pm. To join our community, simply attend our general meetings or reach out to us via email at support@sedstufts.org. Ad astra!
